About Shearline

Time fractures the battlefield into hostile and home territory. Put each character on the ground they can master, then reshape the fight with relics, abilities, and temporal tools that bend the gradient back in your favor. Beneath it all is a world built on stolen years.

A tactical RPG where time is terrain

Shearline is a single-player tactical SRPG with grid-based combat, compact battlefields, temporal terrain that can favor or betray each unit, and party bonds that unlock powerful pairings while creating new risks.

  • Turn-based tacticsCompact battles and full forecasts let you read the board before you commit.
  • Temporal terrainHaste, still, and stabilized ground change movement, pressure, and who controls the fight.
  • Party bondsBonded pairs unlock combo actions, cover each other, and can be punished together when the line breaks.
  • Pixel-art worldDrift stakes, chronolith stations, and stolen years turn each battle into part of a larger frontier mystery.
